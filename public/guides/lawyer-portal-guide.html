Lawyer Portal Guide

This guide walks through everything you can do as a lawyer in your firm's PatienceAI workspace.

1. Activating your account

  1. Your admin invited you by email. Open that email and click Activate & set password.
  2. Pick a strong password and save it somewhere secure (password manager recommended).
  3. You're now logged in at /team/<firm-slug>/lawyer.
Your admin never sees your password. If you forget it, use "Forgot password" or ask your admin to re-send the invite.

2. The tabs you have

My Cases

The matters assigned to you. Create new cases with the same compact "New case" form the admin uses. Edit or delete inline.

Inquiries

When the admin assigns a client inquiry to you, it lands here. You'll also get an email notification. Reply directly from the panel — the email goes to the client and the inquiry flips to "Replied".

Availability

Open consultation slots for clients to book. For in-person slots, set your chamber address once — the client sees it read-only on the booking page.

Consults

Upcoming and past consultations. For video consults, the LiveKit room opens when both you and the client click the link — no install.

Receipts

Issue itemized receipts for retainers / hearing fees / drafting work. Pick the payment method (UPI / NEFT / Cash / Other). If you fill the client email, the PDF auto-emails to them.

Mail

Threaded client emails — connect your Gmail (admin-controlled) and reply from inside the app.

3. Daily routine

  1. Morning: open My Cases, check today's hearings.
  2. Check Inquiries tab — reply to anything new within 24 hours.
  3. Open Consults 10 minutes before each video call to test camera / mic.
  4. After every payment, issue a receipt under Receipts.

4. LawAI assistant

Same floating chat bubble admin gets — but scoped to YOUR cases and inquiries only. Try:

  • "Summarize my open Civil cases."
  • "Draft a follow-up email to the client of case DEL/2026/0142."
  • "How many consultations did I do this month?"

5. Etiquette & best practices

  • Reply to inquiries within 24 hours — it's the #1 conversion driver for new clients.
  • Keep case notes short and dated — they become your story when you need to brief a colleague.
  • Set your availability for the whole week on Monday morning, not slot-by-slot.
  • Issue a receipt the same day a payment lands. Clients remember the polish.
