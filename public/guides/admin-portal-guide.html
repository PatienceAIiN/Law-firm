Admin Portal Guide

This guide walks you through the eleven tabs of your admin panel, from first-day setup to day-to-day operations.

1. First-day setup (10 minutes)

  1. Log in at /team/<your-slug>/admin/login using the email and password from your welcome message.
  2. Branding tab — upload your logo (auto-becomes the browser favicon), pick your colors, set the hero image and "About" headline.
  3. Practice Areas — keep, edit, or remove the four defaults (Corporate / Civil / Criminal / Family). Add your own.
  4. Team — add the firm's team members so they appear on your public site.
  5. Lawyers — invite each lawyer by email. They get an activation link and set their own password.
  6. Account — change your password to something only you know.
Everything you change in the admin panel is live on your public site within seconds — no publish step needed.

2. The eleven tabs

Dashboard

Headline metrics: active cases, new inquiries, revenue MTD, consultations this week. Below them, recent cases and today's calendar.

Practice Areas

Create, reorder, and toggle each practice area on/off. Each area gets its own public page and shows up on the home page card row.

Articles

Long-form posts you publish to your public site (SEO + thought leadership). Use the rich text editor with the same colors as your brand.

Lawyers

Add a lawyer by email — the system emails them an activation link. Never set their password yourself: lawyers set their own password during activation. You can mark a lawyer inactive at any time without deleting their history.

Cases

Track active and closed matters. Each case has a client, an assigned advocate, status, stage, and next-hearing date. Add documents, notes, and activities. Receipts can be linked to a case.

Availability

Open or close consultation slots, per lawyer, per day. Set the in-person chamber address on each slot — your client never has to enter it.

Inquiries

Every contact-form submission lands here, tabbed by status (New / Assigned / Replied / Archived). Assign each inquiry to a lawyer; the lawyer sees it in their own inquiries tab. Reply from inside the app and the response is emailed to the client. Delete with the trash icon.

Receipts

Issue itemized receipts, optionally email the PDF directly to the client. Pick the payment method (UPI / NEFT / Cash / Other) and it shows up on the PDF.

Team

Manage the team-members section of your public site. Different from "Lawyers" — this tab is for the front-facing bios.

Testimonials

Send an "Ask for testimonial" email to a past client. They submit it via a one-time link; you approve before it goes live on the public carousel.

Mail

Connect your Gmail (per workspace) and send threaded conversations from inside the app.

Packages, Branding, Legal, Account

Service packages catalog (shareable via email), branding tokens, Terms / Privacy rich-text editor, and your own password / profile.

3. Day-to-day routine

  1. Open the Dashboard in the morning — see today's hearings and unread inquiries.
  2. Triage Inquiries — assign each one within 24 hours.
  3. Update Cases after every hearing.
  4. Issue Receipts on payment days; the PDF emails itself if you fill the client email.

4. LawAI assistant

The floating chat bubble at the bottom right. Ask: "how many active cases this quarter," "summarize the Acme matter," or "draft a follow-up email to Mr. Bansal." Answers are scoped to your workspace's data only.

5. Troubleshooting

  • Can't log in? Use "Forgot password" on the login page.
  • Image upload fails? Production needs Cloudinary or R2 configured — see the Branding tab for status.
  • Welcome email didn't arrive? Check spam, then ask support to re-send.
